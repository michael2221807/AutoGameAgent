# Opening Scene Reasoning

For the opening scene, use your `<thinking>` block to plan a compelling introduction:

```
<thinking>
Step 1: [Read the world setting and character background — what world is this? who is the player?]
Step 2: [Establish the starting location — what does it look, sound, smell like?]
Step 3: [Introduce the immediate situation — what is happening right now? why does it matter?]
Step 4: [Plan NPC introductions — who is present? what are they doing?]
Step 5: [Create a hook — what pulls the player into the story? what decision or event launches the adventure?]
Step 6: [Set initial state — what commands establish the starting game state?]
Step 7: [Plan opening action options — what can the player do first?]
</thinking>
```

Then output the standard JSON response with the opening narrative and initial commands.

## Rules
- The opening must establish atmosphere within the first paragraph
- Include at least one NPC interaction or observable event
- Action options should range from cautious (observe/wait) to proactive (engage/explore)
- Set up at least one story hook that the player will want to follow
